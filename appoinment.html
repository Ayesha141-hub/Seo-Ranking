Therapy Appointment FAQs

You can reschedule or cancel your therapy session by contacting us at least 24 hours before your appointment time. This allows us to offer the slot to other clients needing support. For cancellations, please call our office or email appointments@zaratherapy.com with your name and appointment details.

Yes, all therapy sessions at Zara Therapy Clinic are strictly confidential and follow professional ethical guidelines. Your personal information and session content are protected under therapist-client privilege, with exceptions only as required by law (such as risk of harm to self or others). We comply with all HIPAA regulations to ensure your privacy.

Yes, we offer secure virtual therapy sessions through our HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform. When booking, select 'Online Session' as your session type. You'll receive a secure link prior to your appointment. Our online therapy services are available for individuals, couples, and families, with the same quality of care as in-person sessions.

For your initial therapy session, we recommend bringing:
  • Completed intake forms (emailed after booking)
  • Insurance card (if using insurance)
  • List of current medications
  • Previous therapy or medical records (if relevant)
  • Notebook for personal reflections
Most importantly, bring an open mind and willingness to engage in the therapeutic process.

Standard individual therapy sessions typically last 50 minutes to one hour. The duration may vary depending on the type of therapy:
  • Initial assessment: 60-90 minutes
  • Individual therapy: 50-60 minutes
  • Couples/family therapy: 60-75 minutes
  • EMDR sessions: 60-90 minutes
Your therapist will discuss the recommended session length based on your treatment plan.
